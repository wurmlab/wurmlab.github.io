Approaches

We believe in Reproducible Research & Software Sustainability. When possible we respect style guides and use unit testing & continuous integration. We develop mostly in:

  • ruby/bioruby (when making pipelines, text & backends;
  • R for number crunching
  • javascript/html/css for client-side stuff and some bioinformatics analysis pipelines
  • But also use bash/python/perl when convenient or necessary...

But we love quick and dirty hacks too .
