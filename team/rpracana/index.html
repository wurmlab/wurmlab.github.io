Rodrigo Pracana Postdoctoral researcher

I am a postdoctoral researcher interested in the evolution of genomes.

LinkedIn   |   Twitter   |   GitHub   |   Scholar

Experience

  • 2013-2018: PhD in Evolutionary Genetics at QMUL: the evolution of the fire ant's social chromosomes
  • 2018-2021: Postdoc at the University of Oxford, with Prof Peter Holland, on the evolution of recombination in gerbils
  • 2021-present: Postdoc at QMUL, on the fire ant's social chromosomes and the effects of pesticides of bee gene expression

Research interests

  • Genome and chromosome evolution
  • Population genetics
  • Genetics of biodiversity

Skills and Experiences

  • Bioinformatics and R
  • Bioinformatics and R teaching in Oxford (2019-2020), QMUL (2013-2017) and at the Swiss Bioinformatics Institute (2016)
  • Conference presentations including Biodiversity Genomics (2020), ESEB (2018), Evolution (2014 and 2018) the Population Genetics Group (2014-2016)
  • Co-organiser of the London Evolutionary Research Network (2014-2016)
