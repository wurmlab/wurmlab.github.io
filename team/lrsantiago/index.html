Leandro Rodrigues Santiago PhD student

I am a PhD student interested in the molecular-genetic mechanisms of ageing. My research project focus on identifying genes responsible for regulating intra-specific differences in lifespan across a broad range of species of social insects.

LinkedIn   |   GitHub   |   Scholar

Research interests

    • Genome evolution
    • Population and comparative genomics
    • Transcriptomics
    • Genetics of ageing

Skills and Experiences

    • Bioinformatics and R
    • Teaching experience at Queen Mary University of London (2014'2018) and at University of Sao Paulo, Brazil (2010'2013)
